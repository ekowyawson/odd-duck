    Welcome to the Product Voting Web App!

    Here is how to use this web app:

    Step 1: Viewing and Voting for Products

    The products displayed are up for voting. Click on any product you like to vote for it. When you click, the app will keep track of your vote.

    Step 2: Maximum Votes

    You can vote a maximum of 20 times, so make your choices wisely. You'll see how many votes you've used as you vote.

    Step 3: Viewing Results

    After reaching the maximum number of votes, a button with the text "View Results" will appear at the bottom. Click the "View Results" button to see the results of the voting. You'll see which products got the most votes.

    Step 4: Understanding the Charts

    The results are shown in charts. You'll see bar charts with different colors. These colors represent votes and views. The taller the bar, the more votes that product received.

    Step 5: Exploring More

    If you want to start over or vote again, simply refresh the web page. Keep voting, enjoy the products, and when you're ready, click "View Results" to see which products were the most popular. Enjoy using the Product Voting Web App!
