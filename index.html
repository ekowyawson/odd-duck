    Welcome to the Picture Voting Web App!

    Here is how to use this web app:

    Step 1: Viewing Products

  • When you open the web app, you'll see products displayed on the screen. These products are up for voting.

    • Step 2: Voting for Products

  • Click on any picture you like to vote for it. When you click, the app will keep track of your vote.

    • Step 3: Maximum Votes

  • You can vote a maximum of 20 times, so make your choices wisely. You'll see how many votes you've used as you vote.

    • Step 4: Viewing Results

  • After reaching the maximum number of votes, a button with the text "View Results" will appear at the bottom.

    • Step 5: Checking Voting Results

  • Click the "View Results" button to see the results of the voting. You'll see which products got the most votes.

    • Step 6: Understanding the Charts

  • The results are shown in charts. You'll see bar charts with different colors.
  • These colors represent votes and views. The taller the bar, the more votes that picture received.

    • Step 7: Exploring More

  • You can also click on products with a specific ID. This doesn't count as a vote but helps you explore the app.

    • Step 8: Enjoy Voting

  • Keep voting, enjoy the products, and when you're ready, click "View Results" to see which products were the most popular.

    • Step 9: Refresh the Page

  • If you want to start over or vote again, simply refresh the web page. Enjoy using the Picture Voting Web App!
